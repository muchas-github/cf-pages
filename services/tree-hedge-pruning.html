What Exactly Is Tree & Hedge Pruning?

Unlike other forms of maintenance – such as trimming or shearing, which may be performed on living leaves and branches – tree pruning services specifically aim to address dead, dying, or decaying growth. This is essential for multiple reasons, from the unsightliness of dead and dying branches, twigs, and leaves to rooting out underlying issues that can have an effect on the overall health of the plant. In instances when a disease is the underlying cause of the decay, effective and efficient tree pruning services are vital to rescuing the rest of the plant.
