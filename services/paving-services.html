Materials Matter: Pro and Con Charts

Below is an overview of the most common landscape paving services in Tacoma that Pierce Landscapers offers.

There are hundreds of varieties of landscape pavers, and this list is far from comprehensive. The major ones we’ll cover below are brick, tile, limestone, slate, granite, and concrete.


Brick

Style and Features

Brick is made from clay and shale, and its color can range from bright red to brown to yellowish-pink. Brick allows for beautiful and versatile brick paving options for any lawn size.

Pros

  • Great for any climate

  • Durable; will last a long time

  • Easy to maintain

Cons

  • Usually more expensive than concrete (but it might look good longer)

  • More limited color options than other pavers

Tile

Style and Features

Tiles give an elegant Mediterranean feel to your patio or landscape. They can come in many beautiful colors and patterns and are perfect for sunny climates.

Pros

  • Stays cool on your feet in hot climates

  • Can come in an infinite amount of patterns and colors

  • Adds an ornate and unique look

Cons

  • Slippery surface, not advised for cold climates

  • Can only be installed properly be a professional

  • Artisan or rare tiles can be hard to replace if broken

  • Can be expensive

Granite

Style and Features

Granite comes in many gorgeous hues and patterns and has very few drawbacks other than price. It’s an excellent choice for any property or climate, and Pierce Landscapers often advises granite paving services in Tacoma.

Pros

  • Extremely tough and durable, doesn’t even chip or scratch

  • Doesn’t fade or lose its color

  • Can be removed and reused

  • Long-lasting use makes it very sustainable

Cons

  • Can be expensive, especially if using a rare color

  • Very heavy, so it cannot be DIYed

Limestone

Style and Features

Limestone comes in many colors, from blue to gray to tan, and can be found all over the world. Limestone paving is versatile and timeless and can look both classic or contemporary.

Pros

  • Reflects heat, excellent for hot climates

  • Long-lasting and tough

  • Perfect for paving around pools

Cons

  • Expensive (but extremely durable)

  • Can erode with acidic cleaners

Slate

Style and Features

Slate comes in many stylish colors, including purple, green, blue, and grey. Its toughness, non-slip surface, and frost resistance make it great for snowy or cold climates.

Pros

  • Perfect for cold climates

  • Durable and weather-resistant

  • Comes in a variety of beautiful colors

Cons

  • Very heavy, usually requires professional installation

  • More expensive than concrete or brick

Cast-In-Place or Precast Concrete

Style and Features

Concrete can be used in any climate and is one of the most cost-effective paving services you can hire. It comes in many shapes and sizes and can be used in any climate.

Precast concrete is molded in a workshop and installed as normal pavers. Cast-in-place concrete is poured on location at your property.

Pros

  • Inexpensive

  • Low-maintenance

  • Perfect for

Cons

  • Can settle unevenly

  • Colored pavers may fade over time

  • Can develop cracks over time

  • Non-recycled concrete is not a very sustainable option

As we said above, this list of common paving services is not comprehensive. Make sure you do research into other types of stone like bluestone, travertine, gravel, crushed stone, and more.
